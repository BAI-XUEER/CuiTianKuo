COURSE REFLECTION

I. Initial Motivation

My decision to enroll in this leadership course stemmed primarily from considerations about my future career development. I recognize that leadership is a core competency every individual should possess, regardless of the industry. I sought to move beyond "giving orders" to gain a systematic understanding of authentic leadership.

II. Content Review & Reflection

I have learned that leadership is fundamentally the ability to influence others, inspire shared goals, and drive their realization. It is built upon trust and vision rather than hierarchical position.

Dimensions of Transformational Leadership

Idealized Influence Becoming a role model through noble character and earning team trust.
Inspirational Motivation Igniting team passion by articulating a clear and compelling vision.
Intellectual Stimulation Encouraging innovative thinking and challenging the status quo.
Individualized Consideration Attending to each member's growth needs through support and guidance.
"I no longer view leadership as an innate talent but as a capability that can be developed through learning, practice, and reflection."
III. Conclusion and Outlook

Moving forward, I plan to focus on listening, building trust, and inspiring shared purpose. I am confident these concepts will empower me to become a leader who achieves personal success while enabling others to reach their full potential.
